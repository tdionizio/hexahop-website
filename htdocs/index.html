Hex-a-Hop 1.1.0 released!

We've finally finished the 1.1.0 release of Hex-a-Hop which features new sound support, music, and sound effects. The gameplay is the same classic game you're used to. To play it, there's no need for installation -- just download and unzip it. Enjoy!

New Website Now Online!

We wanted to bring new life to Tom Beaumont's excellent game Hex-a-Hop by adding some music and sounds. Of course, we also needed a website for the new release -- a website on par with the original and beautiful style of the game. Luckily we found Bart Kelsey to put together some art and design for our superb new website. Thanks a ton, Bart!
